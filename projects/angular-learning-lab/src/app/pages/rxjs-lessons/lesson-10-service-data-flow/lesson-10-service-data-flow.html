What To Remember

  1. A service can own the Subjects, operators, backend calls, and shared view-model stream.
  2. The component can call service methods instead of knowing about next(), switchMap, or catchError.
  3. A component-level provider creates a fresh service instance for this screen.
  4. This pattern is a lightweight cousin of a facade: expose useful streams and useful methods.
