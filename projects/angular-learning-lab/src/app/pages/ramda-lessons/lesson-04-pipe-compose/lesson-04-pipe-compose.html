When To Reach For Ramda

Prefer the clearest flow

Plain named steps are often easiest. Use pipe when the repeated left-to-right shape makes the business flow easier to scan.

What To Remember

  1. pipe(a, b, c)(value) runs a, then b, then c.
  2. compose(c, b, a)(value) produces the same order, but reads right-to-left.
  3. Use named functions like removeArchived so the pipeline explains the business steps.
  4. If the pipeline becomes hard to read, split it into smaller named functions or use plain TypeScript.
