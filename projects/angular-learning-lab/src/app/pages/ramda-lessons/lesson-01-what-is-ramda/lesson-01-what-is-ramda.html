Ramda Lesson 1

What Is Ramda?

Ramda is a utility library for transforming plain JavaScript data. It is not tied to Angular, NgRx, RxJS, or forms. You can use it anywhere you need small pure functions for filtering, sorting, composing, or updating immutable data.

What To Remember

  1. Ramda is a general data-transformation library, not an NgRx feature.
  2. Most Ramda helpers take data and return new data.
  3. Pure transformations are easier to reuse in components, services, selectors, and streams.
  4. Ramda is helpful only when the result is easier to read than plain TypeScript.
