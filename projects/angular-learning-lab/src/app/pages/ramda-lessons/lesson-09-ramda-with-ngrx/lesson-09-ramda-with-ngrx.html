What To Remember

  1. NgRx owns the architecture: actions, reducers, selectors, effects, and store flow.
  2. Ramda is just a helper library for immutable data transformations.
  3. Use Ramda when it removes noisy spread syntax or clarifies transformation steps.
  4. Avoid Ramda when the helper chain hides simple business logic.
  5. In real teams, consistency matters more than using a clever helper everywhere.
