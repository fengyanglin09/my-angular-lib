What To Remember

  1. afterNextRender runs once after the next completed render.
  2. afterRenderEffect tracks signals and runs after render when they change.
  3. Use the write phase for DOM writes and the read phase for DOM measurements.
  4. Avoid using these hooks for normal derived state; use computed for that.
