What To Remember

  1. updateOn: 'change' validates from user input as the user types.
  2. updateOn: 'blur' waits until the field loses focus.
  3. updateOn: 'submit' waits until the form is submitted.
  4. Programmatic updates like patchValue still update controls immediately.
  5. Use blur or submit when instant validation would be noisy or expensive.
