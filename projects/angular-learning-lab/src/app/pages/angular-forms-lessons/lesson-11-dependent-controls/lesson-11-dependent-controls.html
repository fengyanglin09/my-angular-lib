What To Remember

  1. Put dependent-control rules in component code, not scattered across template conditions.
  2. Use enable and disable when a field should join or leave the form payload.
  3. Use setValidators and clearValidators when requirements change.
  4. Call updateValueAndValidity after changing validators.
  5. emitEvent: true announces internal updates to valueChanges and statusChanges.
  6. emitEvent: false makes internal adjustments quietly, keeping the original user change as the main event.
