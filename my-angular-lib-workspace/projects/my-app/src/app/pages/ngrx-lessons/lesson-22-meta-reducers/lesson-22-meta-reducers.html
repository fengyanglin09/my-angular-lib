Meta-Reducers And Persistence

A meta-reducer wraps normal reducers. In this lesson, the reducer updates preferences, while the meta-reducer saves and restores that slice from localStorage.

What Is Happening

  1. The component dispatches normal preference actions.
  2. The reducer returns the next preference state.
  3. The meta-reducer sees that preference action and saves the slice.
  4. On app startup, the meta-reducer hydrates the slice from localStorage.
  5. The component does not contain persistence logic.
