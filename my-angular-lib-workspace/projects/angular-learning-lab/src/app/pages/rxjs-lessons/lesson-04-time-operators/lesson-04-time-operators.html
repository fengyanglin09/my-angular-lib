Time Operators

Time operators control when values move through a stream. The source may emit right away, but operators like delay, debounceTime, and throttleTime decide when the subscriber is allowed to receive those values.

What To Remember

  1. delay shifts delivery later, but it does not stop the source from emitting.
  2. debounceTime is best when you only care about the latest value after a pause.
  3. throttleTime is best when you want the first value, then a short cooldown.
  4. The source Observable and the subscriber can experience different timing because operators sit between them.
