What To Inspect In Store DevTools

  1. Click Increment and find [DevTools Lab] Increment Counter in the action list.
  2. Open the State tab and look for the devtoolsLab feature state.
  3. Open the Diff tab after Set label to see only label change.
  4. Click Load audit trail and watch one action turn into a later success action from the effect.
  5. Click Reset, then use time travel to compare before and after states.
  6. For Trace, reload the page, click DevTools Reset, then dispatch a new action. Trace does not appear for old actions from before tracing was enabled.
